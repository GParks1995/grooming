Our Grooming Services

We offer three core packages tailored to your dog’s needs. Add-ons let you customise the experience even further.

Bath & Brush

A gentle bath using premium shampoos followed by a thorough blow dry and brush-out. Includes ear cleaning and nail trim to keep your pup fresh between full grooms.

Bath & Tidy

Everything in the Bath & Brush plus trimming of the face, feet and sanitary areas. Perfect for maintaining your dog’s coat and keeping them looking tidy between haircuts.

Cut & Style

A complete grooming session with a full-body haircut tailored to your dog’s breed or your preferred style. Includes bath, blow dry, brush-out and finishing touches.
